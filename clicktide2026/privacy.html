Clicktide Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 8, 2026
Clicktide is operated by Six Seasons Partners LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how Clicktide collects, uses, shares, and protects information.

1. Information we collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account, including name, email, phone number, business name, business contact details, address, billing selections, campaign settings, uploaded files, logos, and support messages.

We may collect customer information that you upload or sync from connected platforms, such as customer names, emails, phone numbers, purchase history, visit history, order information, loyalty status, campaign eligibility, and gift delivery details.

2. Information from connected services

When you connect a point-of-sale, commerce, payment, scheduling, email, SMS, or fulfillment provider, we receive information allowed by that provider and authorized by you. This may include transaction, customer, subscription, product, appointment, or campaign data needed to operate Clicktide.

3. How we use information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To operate customer messaging, retention, loyalty, win-back, and gift campaigns.
  • To process checkout, subscriptions, invoices, usage, gift wallet activity, and billing.
  • To connect with third-party platforms you authorize.
  • To provide analytics, ROI estimates, campaign recommendations, and support.
  • To protect the service, prevent abuse, troubleshoot errors, and comply with law.

4. Customer data responsibilities

Your business is responsible for having the required rights, consent, notice, or lawful basis to upload, sync, and use customer information with Clicktide. You are also responsible for honoring customer opt-outs and communication preferences where required.

5. SMS and text message data

If a business uses Clicktide to send SMS or text messages, we may process customer mobile numbers, consent status, opt-in timestamps, message content, delivery status, replies, opt-out requests, and related carrier or provider records. Text messages may be sent through providers such as Twilio.

Businesses using Clicktide may send text messages to customers who have given express consent to receive them. Message types may include loyalty and appreciation messages, satisfaction surveys, win-back check-ins, gift notifications, and delivery updates. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

Businesses are responsible for collecting valid consent before sending text messages. Customers may opt out by replying STOP, or request help by replying HELP where supported. No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We do not sell customer mobile numbers, and we do not share mobile opt-in data or consent records with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing purposes.

6. Sharing information

We share information with service providers that help operate Clicktide, including hosting, database, payment processing, email, SMS, analytics, platform integrations, shipping, and fulfillment providers. We may also share information when required by law, to protect rights and safety, or in connection with a business transfer.

7. Payments

Payment information is processed by Stripe or another payment processor. Clicktide does not store full payment card numbers. Payment processors handle payment data under their own terms and privacy policies.

8. Data retention

We retain information as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal and accounting obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain security. You may request deletion of account data, subject to lawful retention requirements and technical limitations.

9. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your choices

You can update account information in the dashboard, disconnect integrations, stop campaigns, request export or deletion where available, and contact us for privacy questions. Customers who receive messages from a business should contact that business or use the opt-out method provided in the message.

11. Children's privacy

Clicktide is not intended for children under 13, and businesses should not upload children's personal information unless they have all required legal authority and safeguards.

12. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. If changes are material, we will take reasonable steps to notify users, such as posting an updated date or providing in-app notice.

13. Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to support@goclicktide.com.
