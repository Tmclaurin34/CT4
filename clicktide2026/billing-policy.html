Clicktide Billing Policy

Effective date: June 8, 2026
Clicktide is operated by Six Seasons Partners LLC. This Billing Policy explains subscriptions, wallet funds, cancellations, refunds, failed payments, and billing disputes.

1. Plans and recurring billing

Clicktide offers paid subscription plans such as Local, Growth, and Scale. By selecting a paid plan and completing checkout, you authorize Clicktide and its payment processor to charge your payment method on a recurring basis until you cancel.

New subscriptions begin with a 30-day free trial. A payment method is collected at checkout, but no subscription charge is made during the trial. Your first subscription charge occurs when the trial ends, and recurring billing continues from that date until you cancel. Wallet top-ups and wallet-funded gift activity are separate purchases and are charged when made, including during a trial.

2. Gift wallet and usage charges

Subscription fees cover platform access and included features described at checkout or in the dashboard. Physical gifts, shipping, taxes, premium fulfillment, add-ons, overages, or wallet-funded activity may cost extra. Gift wallet funds are used to pay for approved gift activity and related fulfillment costs.

Gift wallet funds are credited to the specific business account that purchased them and are not transferable between unrelated businesses unless approved by Clicktide. Wallet funds may be reduced when a gift, message, shipping label, tax, fulfillment action, or related campaign expense is authorized or processed.

3. Taxes

Prices may not include taxes, duties, or government charges. Where required, taxes may be calculated and charged by our payment processor.

4. Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time through the dashboard or by contacting support. If you cancel during your free trial, you will not be charged a subscription fee. After the trial, cancellation stops future subscription renewals, but it does not automatically refund charges already incurred. Access may continue until the end of the current paid billing period unless otherwise stated.

5. Refunds

Subscription fees are generally non-refundable unless required by law or expressly approved by Clicktide. Gift, fulfillment, shipping, and usage charges may be non-refundable once a campaign, order, message, or fulfillment action has started. If we issue a refund, it does not create an obligation to issue the same refund in the future.

Unused gift wallet balances that have not been committed to campaigns, orders, fulfillment, shipping, taxes, disputes, chargebacks, or payment processor activity may be eligible for refund at Clicktide's discretion or where required by law.

6. Failed payments

If a payment fails, we may retry the charge, notify you, restrict paid features, pause campaigns, or suspend account access until payment is resolved. You remain responsible for amounts owed.

7. Plan changes

If you upgrade, downgrade, or change plans, billing may be prorated or adjusted depending on payment processor settings and the effective date of the change. Some features may be unavailable after a downgrade.

8. Disputes

If you believe a charge is incorrect, contact us promptly at support@goclicktide.com. You agree to work with us in good faith before initiating a chargeback where possible.

9. Contact

Billing questions can be sent to support@goclicktide.com.
