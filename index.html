How Clicktide wins back customers

Almost no one cancels at the front desk — they just stop coming in. Clicktide connects to your point-of-sale (Square, Shopify, Clover, Toast, Stripe and more), learns each customer's normal visit rhythm, and acts the moment someone breaks it:

  • Day 14 — a friendly text or email. A personal check-in that references their real last visit. No pressure.
  • Day 30 — a real mailed postcard. Printed in your brand color and logo and sent to their door.
  • Day 45 — a small branded gift. A mug, tote, or candle — only when a regular is genuinely about to lapse, and only with your approval.

The moment a customer comes back in, every future win-back message stops automatically. Three touches maximum, then it's done.

A layer on top of your gym or salon software — not a replacement

Common questions

Will this annoy or spam my customers?

No — three win-back touches maximum, then it stops for good. Any visit stops all future sends automatically. Texts go only to customers with consent on file, and approve-before-send means nothing goes out unseen.

What can Clicktide see in my register? Is it safe?

Read-only access through your platform's official connection. Clicktide can see visit and sales history to spot drifting — it can't move money or change your account, and you can disconnect in one click.

How is this different from Mailchimp or email blasts?

Email tools broadcast to everybody and hope. Clicktide watches your register and acts on one person at the right moment, then goes quiet the instant they return. It's a win-back engine, not a newsletter.
